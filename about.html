About Wander AI

Wander AI is a smart tourism platform that combines Artificial Intelligence and Virtual Reality to enhance the travel planning experience. Our system provides:

  • ✅ Personalized AI-based destination recommendations
  • ✅ An interactive AI chatbot for travel queries
  • ✅ Immersive VR experiences of destinations

This project aims to make tourism smarter, more interactive, and accessible for everyone.
